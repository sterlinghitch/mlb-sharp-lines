How the Enhanced Model Works
Step 1 - Market true odds: Vig stripped and averaged across FD, DK, MGM, BetOnline, MyBookie, BetUS. Outliers excluded.
Step 2 - SP adjustment: Each starter ERA vs league avg (4.20) shifts win probability up to +/-4%.
Step 3 - Bullpen fatigue: Relief IP last 3 days -- fatigued pen shifts opponent probability up to +/-4%.
Step 4 - Injury penalty: Key position players OUT shift win probability ~2% per player.
Step 5 - Park factor: Applied to Over/Under true probability (Coors +28%, Petco -9%, etc).
Step 6 - Umpire zone: Run impact from UmpScorecards shifts Over/Under probability 1% per run unit.
Step 7 - Wind: 8+ mph blowing out/in shifts total probability up to +/-6%.
Step 8 - Best bet: Away ML, home ML, and Over/Under all scored by edge -- highest wins.

How HR probability is calculated
